Where it all started

Founded in the heart of Ankara in 2024, Macarooon began with a simple dream — to share the joy that a single bite of something sweet can bring. What started as a small kitchen experiment between friends who loved macarons soon turned into a cozy bakery filled with the smell of freshly baked treats and laughter.

At Macarooon, we believe that every dessert tells a story. Each pastry, cookie, and cake we make is crafted with care, using recipes that blend traditional techniques with a modern touch. From hand-whipped creams to colorful macarons inspired by the seasons, we pour our hearts into every creation that leaves our oven.

Our mission is simple — to make every visitor feel at home. Whether you’re stopping by for your morning croissant, celebrating a special moment with a custom cake, or just craving a sweet break in the middle of your day, we’re here to make it memorable.

Ready to try? Order online through our Instagram, or reserve a delightful spread for your next event, and let us make your occasion extra special. Macarooon — where every bite is a memory waiting to be made.
